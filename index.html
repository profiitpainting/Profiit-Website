How We Work

A Proven Process From Walkthrough to Closeout.

PROFiiT uses a simple but disciplined project flow to keep teams aligned and projects moving.

01

Discovery & Site Review

We review the property, project goals, existing conditions, access points, stakeholder needs, schedule requirements, and risk areas.

02

Scope & Estimate Development

We define the painting scope, coating needs, surface preparation, labor assumptions, phasing, materials, and proposal structure.

03

Pre-Production Planning

Before mobilization, we align schedule, communication, manpower, staging, safety expectations, resident or tenant impact, and site logistics.

04

Field Execution

Crews perform the work under defined production expectations, quality standards, and management oversight.

05

Progress Communication

Stakeholders receive clear updates on production, schedule, field conditions, changes, and next steps.

06

Punch, Closeout & Documentation

We complete final walkthroughs, address punch items, document completion, and close the project professionally.

Our Process

Our Process Keeps Painting Projects Controlled.

PROFiiT uses a structured project workflow so owners, managers, contractors, residents, tenants, and field teams stay aligned from the first walkthrough to final closeout.

1

Consultation

We identify the property type, project drivers, timeline, decision-makers, budget considerations, and known concerns.

2

Site Walkthrough

We inspect project areas, surface conditions, access needs, staging constraints, tenant or resident impact, and logistical challenges.

3

Scope Definition

We clarify inclusions, exclusions, coating systems, preparation standards, phasing, alternates, and assumptions.

4

Proposal & Planning

We develop a proposal that supports informed decision-making and creates a practical path for production.

5

Pre-Construction Coordination

We align communication, schedule, manpower, safety, staging, notifications, and documentation before work begins.

6

Production Management

Field teams execute the work under defined expectations while leadership monitors quality, progress, and project needs.

7

Closeout

We complete walkthroughs, manage punch items, document finished areas, and close the project with accountability.
